## What Happens Next

When you submit this task, Mariposa will:

1. **Acknowledge receipt** of your task
2. **Route to the correct agent(s)** based on the task type
3. **Flag anything that needs your review** per the Approval Matrix
4. **Produce the requested deliverable** or coordinate agent work
5. **Return results** in the standard format unless you request otherwise

---

## Quick Tips

- **Be specific** in the Final Deliverable field
- **Set realistic deadlines** - rush work affects quality
- **Flag approval needs early** - don't surprise yourself later
- **Include all relevant links** - saves time and ensures accuracy
- **Use the Notes field** for context that doesn't fit elsewhere
