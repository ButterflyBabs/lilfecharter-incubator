# Next Faithful Step Protocol

**Standard Closing for All Task Responses**

*Created: May 9, 2026*
*Last Updated: May 9, 2026*

---

## Purpose

Every task response, routing decision, or completed deliverable must end with a clear, single next action for Babs. This ensures momentum and eliminates decision fatigue.

---

## The Rule

**When a task is routed or completed, always end with:**

```
NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
State the one next action Babs should take.
```

---

## Guidelines

### Make It:
- **Single** - One action only
- **Specific** - Clear what to do
- **Actionable** - Something Babs can actually do
- **Sized** - Small enough to complete in one sitting
- **Faithful** - Aligned with the work just completed

### Avoid:
- Multiple steps (that's a plan, not a next step)
- Vague suggestions ("think about it")
- Overwhelming scope ("redesign everything")
- Passive voice ("it should be reviewed")
- Delegation without clarity ("someone should...")

---

## Examples

### Good Next Faithful Steps:

✅ "Review the email draft and reply 'approved' or with edits"

✅ "Click this link to test the landing page: [URL]"

✅ "Schedule 30 minutes tomorrow to review the curriculum outline"

✅ "Reply with the speaker's confirmed email address"

✅ "Approve the social post by replying 'post it'"

### Poor Next Faithful Steps:

❌ "Think about the strategy and let me know your thoughts" (too vague)

❌ "Review the document, edit it, send it to the team, and schedule a meeting" (too many steps)

❌ "The funnel should be tested" (passive, not actionable)

❌ "We need to redesign the entire email sequence" (overwhelming scope)

---

## Integration with Response Format

The NEXT FAITHFUL STEP is the final field in the standard response:

```
TASK RECEIVED:
[Brief restatement]

BUSINESS AREA:
[Identification]

PRIMARY AGENT:
[Agent name]

SUPPORT AGENTS:
[Supporting agents]

APPROVAL REQUIRED:
[Yes/No with reason]

MISSING INFORMATION:
[What is needed]

RECOMMENDED NEXT STEP:
[What should happen next]

FINAL DELIVERABLE:
[What will be produced]

NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
[The one action Babs should take now]
```

---

## Special Cases

### When Approval Is Required:
```
NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
Review the attached draft and reply "approved" or provide edits by [time].
```

### When Information Is Missing:
```
NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
Provide the missing speaker email address so I can complete the outreach draft.
```

### When Task Is Complete:
```
NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
Test the live landing page at [URL] and confirm everything works as expected.
```

### When Multiple Options:
```
NEXT FAITHFUL STEP:
Choose Option A or Option B and reply with your selection.
```

---

## The Principle

Babs should never be left wondering "what now?" The NEXT FAITHFUL STEP is the bridge between the work just done and the work about to begin. It honors her time, reduces cognitive load, and keeps momentum flowing.

---

*One step. Clear action. Faithful execution.*
