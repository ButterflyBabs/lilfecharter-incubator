## Usage Notes

1. **Fill out completely** - Every field matters for proper routing
2. **Be specific** - Vague tasks lead to vague results
3. **Flag missing info early** - Don't wait until halfway through
4. **One task per form** - Don't combine unrelated tasks
5. **Update as needed** - If requirements change, update the form
