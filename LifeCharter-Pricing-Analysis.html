Conclusion

The recommended pricing restructure makes LifeCharter Architecture competitive while preserving its premium positioning:

  • 33-67% price reduction across tiers improves accessibility
  • Clear value ladder from Foundation to Enterprise
  • Setup fees maintained to ensure committed customers
  • Unique differentiation (12-domain alignment, AI Guide) justifies premium over commodity tools
  • Annual pricing improves cash flow and reduces churn

Expected Outcome: 3-5x increase in trial signups, improved conversion rates, and maintained or increased revenue through volume growth.
