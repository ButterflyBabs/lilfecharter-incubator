# Sacred Kaleidoscope Community

### May 2026 - July 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This 90-day plan is designed to create consistent momentum for Sacred Kaleidoscope Community by focusing on five core objectives:
1. **Grow the email list** with aligned, spiritually-curious subscribers
2. **Fill LifeCharter Incubator workshops** with qualified participants
3. **Convert Incubator attendees into LifeCharter Circle members**
4. **Secure speaking opportunities** through AmiLynne Speaks
5. **Promote Eva Mae Foundation and the Becoming Uncaged GoFundMe campaign** (goal: $10K+)

The strategy prioritizes relationship over volume, sacred clarity over hype, and sustainable systems over burnout.

The current focus is funding "Becoming Uncaged: A Map for the Middle of It" — a book/workbook designed to be a "travel partner in paper form" for people in hard conditions, especially incarceration.

### Campaign Goal

- Editing and design costs
- Initial print run

- Supporting correspondence ministry infrastructure

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges:
1. **Low Incubator registration:** Have backup promotion plan, partner outreach
2. **Low Circle conversion:** Adjust offer positioning, add payment plans
3. **GoFundMe not reaching goal:** Extend timeline, adjust messaging, explore grants
4. **Content burnout:** Build buffer content, use repurposing system
5. **Tech issues:** Document workarounds, have backup communication methods

### Contingency Plans:
- Maintain 2-week content buffer
- Have simplified offer ready if full Circle doesn't fill
- Build relationships with backup speakers/collaborators
- Keep manual processes documented for all automations
- Identify 3-5 potential grant opportunities for Eva Mae Foundation
