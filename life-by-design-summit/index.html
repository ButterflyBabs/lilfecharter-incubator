Frequently Asked Questions

Is the summit really free?

Yes. Free registration gives you access to attend the summit live.

When is the summit?

The summit takes place online July 23, 24, and 25, 2026 from 9am - 2pm MT each day.

Will recordings be available?

Limited-time replay access is included with the Next Chapter Pass. Extended access to all summit recordings is included with the Integration Pass.

Who is this summit for?

This summit is for thoughtful, growth-minded people who are ready to move from overwhelm and uncertainty into clarity, purpose, and aligned action.

Do I need to be familiar with LifeCharter before attending?

No. The summit is designed to introduce you to the LifeCharter framework in a clear, accessible, and practical way.

What are the pass options?

You may choose the Free Summit Registration, the Next Chapter Pass, or the Integration Pass, depending on how much access, support, and integration you want during and after the summit.

Will there be a pitch?

There may be invitations to continue the journey through LifeCharter offerings, but the summit itself is designed to provide real value, reflection, and practical next steps.
