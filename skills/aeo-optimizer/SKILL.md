## Guardrails
- **No Keyword Stuffing:** Ensure content remains natural and readable; AI engines penalize keyword stuffing just like traditional search engines.
- **Focus on Facts:** Prioritize accuracy and factual information over marketing fluff; AI engines look for reliable data to cite.
- **Maintain Clear Structure:** Always use logical heading hierarchies (H1, H2, H3) and short, scannable paragraphs.
- **Validate Schema:** Ensure all generated schema markup is valid and error-free before implementation.
- **Prioritize User Intent:** Always optimize for the user's underlying question or need, not just the search algorithm.
- **Avoid Plagiarism:** Ensure all content is original and properly attributes external sources.
- **Don't Guarantee Rankings:** Manage expectations; AI search algorithms are complex and constantly evolving.
- **Stay Updated:** Continuously monitor changes in AI search engine behavior and adjust strategies accordingly.

## Failure handling

| Error Scenario | Cause | User-Friendly Message |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Content not cited by AI | Lack of authority signals or unclear structure | 🤔 "It seems AI engines aren't citing your content yet. Let's try to improve its clarity and boost its authority by adding expert quotes and getting more positive reviews." |
| Schema markup errors | Invalid JSON-LD syntax or missing required fields | ❌ "Looks like there's an error in the schema code. I can use a special tool to find and fix it for you." |
| AI hallucinates brand info | Weak entity recognition or conflicting information online | 🤯 "The AI seems confused about your brand. Let's make sure your business name, address, and phone number are consistent everywhere online and build a stronger brand profile." |
| Traffic drops after optimization | Over-optimization or ignoring traditional SEO factors | 📉 "It seems your traffic has dropped a bit. This can happen if we focus too much on AI and not enough on traditional SEO. Let's make sure your content is still super user-friendly." |
| Competitors outranking | Competitors have stronger authority or better-structured data | 🔭 "Your competitors are ranking higher right now. I'll do a deep dive to see what they're doing differently and find opportunities for you to get ahead." |
| Local business not recommended | Incomplete or inconsistent local profiles | 🗺️ "Your local business isn't being recommended yet. Let's make sure your Google Business Profile, Yelp page, and other local listings are complete and consistent." |
| AI engine ignores FAQ section | Missing or invalid FAQPage schema | ❓ "The AI is ignoring your FAQ section. I'll double-check that the special FAQ schema code is set up correctly." |
| Content deemed "unhelpful" | Too much marketing fluff, lack of direct answers | ✍️ "Your content might have too much marketing language. Let's rewrite it to be more direct, factual, and helpful to the user." |

💡 **Pro tip:** AEO is a marathon, not a sprint. It can take time for AI engines to recognize and trust your content. Be patient and consistent!
