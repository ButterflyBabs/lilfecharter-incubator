# Idea Generator Skill

Generates business ideas, content ideas, and simple offers based on a user's topic, niche, or business.

---

## Trigger

`/idea generator`

---

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Ask the User

Prompt:
> "What topic, niche, or business are you interested in?"

Wait for their response.

---

### Step 2 — Generate Ideas

Based on their answer, create:

#### SECTION 1: Business Ideas
Create **10 simple business ideas** related to the topic.

#### SECTION 2: Content Ideas  
Create **10 engaging content ideas** (social media, videos, posts).

#### SECTION 3: Simple Offers
Create **3 offers** they could sell immediately.

Each offer should include:
- **Name**
- **What it includes**
- **Suggested price**

---

### Step 3 — Format Guidelines

Keep everything:
- ✅ Simple
- ✅ Clear
- ✅ Beginner-friendly
- ✅ Actionable

Avoid complicated language or jargon.

## Safety Rules

- ✅ Keep suggestions legal and ethical
- ✅ Make prices realistic for beginners
- ✅ Focus on actionable, low-barrier ideas
- ❌ Avoid MLM schemes or get-rich-quick promises
- ❌ Don't suggest illegal business practices
