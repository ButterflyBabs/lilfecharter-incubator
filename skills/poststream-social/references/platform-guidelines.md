# Platform-Specific Social Media Guidelines

This reference provides detailed guidelines for creating effective content on each social media platform.

## Twitter/X

### Character Limits
- Standard tweet: 280 characters
- Twitter Blue subscribers: up to 25,000 characters

### Best Practices
- Keep it concise and punchy
- Use hashtags strategically (1-2 per tweet)
- Tag relevant accounts with @mentions
- Include visuals for 150% more engagement
- Post threads for longer stories
- Best times: Weekdays 8-10 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM

### Content Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Include CTAs (calls to action)
- Use emojis to add personality
- Break up long threads with visual content

### Media Support
- Images: up to 4 per tweet (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Videos: up to 2 minutes 20 seconds (140 seconds)
- GIFs: supported
- Optimal image size: 1200 x 675 pixels

---

## LinkedIn

### Character Limits
- Post text: 3,000 characters (first 140 visible in feed)
- Article title: 100 characters
- Article text: 110,000 characters

### Best Practices
- Front-load important content (first 140 characters)
- Professional yet conversational tone
- Use line breaks for readability
- Ask thought-provoking questions
- Share insights and expertise
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday 7-9 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM

### Content Guidelines
- Focus on professional development and industry insights
- Use storytelling to humanize your brand
- Share case studies and success stories
- Include data and statistics when relevant
- Tag companies and people appropriately

### Media Support
- Images: Single image per post
- Videos: up to 10 minutes
- Documents: PDFs, PowerPoint (up to 100 MB)
- Optimal image size: 1200 x 627 pixels

---

## Facebook

### Character Limits
- Post text: 63,206 characters
- Practically unlimited for most use cases

### Best Practices
- Keep posts under 250 characters for best engagement
- Use Facebook Live for real-time engagement
- Create events for upcoming activities
- Use Facebook Stories for timely content
- Best times: Weekdays 1-3 PM, Thursday-Friday 1-4 PM

### Content Guidelines
- Encourage conversation with questions
- Use emotional storytelling
- Share user-generated content
- Create shareable content (tips, lists, how-tos)
- Use Facebook Insights to optimize posting times

### Media Support
- Images: Multiple images in carousel
- Videos: up to 240 minutes
- 360° photos and videos supported
- Optimal image size: 1200 x 630 pixels

---

## Instagram

### Character Limits
- Caption: 2,200 characters
- Bio: 150 characters
- Username: 30 characters

### Best Practices
- Visual content is REQUIRED (photo or video)
- First line of caption is critical (125 characters visible)
- Use 11-30 hashtags for maximum reach
- Tag location when relevant
- Post consistently (at least 3-7 times per week)
- Best times: Weekdays 6 AM, 12 PM, 7-9 PM

### Content Guidelines
- High-quality visuals are essential
- Use Instagram Stories for behind-the-scenes content
- Create Reels for higher reach
- Use carousel posts for tutorials and step-by-step content
- Write engaging captions with strong CTAs

### Media Support
- Photos: Square (1080 x 1080), Portrait (1080 x 1350), Landscape (1080 x 566)
- Videos: Up to 60 seconds (Feed), 90 seconds (Reels)
- Stories: 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio)
- Carousel: up to 10 images or videos

### Hashtag Strategy
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- Create branded hashtags
- Research hashtags before using
- Place hashtags at the end of caption or in first comment

---

## TikTok

### Character Limits
- Video caption: 2,200 characters (150 recommended)
- Bio: 80 characters
- Username: 24 characters

### Best Practices
- Video content is REQUIRED
- First 3 seconds are critical (hook viewers immediately)
- Use trending sounds and music
- Participate in challenges and trends
- Post 1-4 times per day for best results
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday 7-9 AM, 7-11 PM

### Content Guidelines
- Authentic and relatable content performs best
- Vertical video format (9:16 ratio)
- Use on-screen text for accessibility
- Jump cuts and fast pacing work well
- Engage with comments quickly

### Media Support
- Video length: 15 seconds to 10 minutes
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 pixels (vertical)
- File size: up to 287.6 MB (iOS), 72 MB (Android)
- Format: MP4 or MOV recommended

### Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 hashtags optimal
- Mix trending and niche hashtags
- Include #FYP or #ForYou for discovery
- Create challenge hashtags

---

## YouTube

### Character Limits
- Video title: 100 characters
- Description: 5,000 characters
- Tags: 500 characters total

### Best Practices
- Thumbnails are critical for CTR
- Front-load important keywords in title and description
- Use chapters/timestamps for longer videos
- Create playlists to increase watch time
- Consistent upload schedule
- Best times: Thursday-Saturday 12-3 PM

### Content Guidelines
- First 15 seconds determine viewer retention
- Include clear CTAs (subscribe, like, comment)
- Use end screens to promote other videos
- Optimize video descriptions with keywords
- Engage with comments to boost algorithm favor

### Media Support
- Video length: Up to 12 hours (verified accounts)
- Resolution: up to 8K
- Optimal: 1920 x 1080 (1080p) at 60 fps
- Aspect ratios: 16:9 (standard), 9:16 (Shorts)

---

## Pinterest

### Character Limits
- Pin description: 500 characters
- Board name: 180 characters
- Board description: 500 characters

### Best Practices
- Vertical images perform best (2:3 ratio)
- Use keyword-rich descriptions
- Create multiple boards for different topics
- Pin consistently (5-30 pins per day)
- Best times: Saturday 8-11 PM

### Content Guidelines
- High-quality, inspiring images
- Add text overlay to images
- Use rich pins for enhanced information
- Link to valuable content
- Optimize for search (Pinterest is a visual search engine)

### Media Support
- Images: PNG or JPG
- Optimal size: 1000 x 1500 pixels (2:3 ratio)
- Videos: 4 seconds to 15 minutes
- File size: up to 20 MB (video), 32 MB (image)

## General Tips for All Platforms

1. **Know your audience** - Tailor content to each platform's demographic
2. **Timing matters** - Post when your audience is most active
3. **Consistency is key** - Regular posting builds audience trust
4. **Engage authentically** - Respond to comments and messages
5. **Track performance** - Use analytics to refine your strategy
6. **Repurpose content** - Adapt content for each platform's format
7. **Use native features** - Platforms favor content using their latest features
8. **Quality over quantity** - Better to post less often with high-quality content
9. **Test and iterate** - Experiment with different formats and posting times
10. **Stay current** - Keep up with platform updates and trending formats
