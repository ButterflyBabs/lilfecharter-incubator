## Brand Guidelines

### Brand Feel
Sacred, precise, calm, premium, emotionally resonant, warm, contemplative, spacious, symbolic, grounded, human.

**Design rule:** air before noise. Use one focal message per frame. Let the work breathe.

**Creative rule:** Use Ivory Light or Paper instead of pure white. Use Deep Indigo or Ink instead of pure black. Gold accents should be elegant filigree, not overwhelming. Let the work breathe with generous negative space.

### Typography

#### LifeCharter Brand Typography (Current - May 2026)
*Based on the LifeCharter Brand Board - elegant, editorial, refined*

- **Primary Display:** An Elegant Classic Serif - headlines, hero statements, major titles
- **Secondary Serif:** A Refined Editorial Serif - section headers, subheads, emphasis
- **Accent Italic:** A Graceful Serif Italic - pull quotes, signature statements, poetic moments
- **Supporting Text:** A Clean, Readable Sans Serif - body copy, labels, data, CTAs, captions

**Fallbacks:** Georgia for serif, Arial for sans
**Rule:** never use more than two font families in one asset. EB Garamond and Open Sans remain acceptable for existing materials.

### Canva + Social Sizing
- Feed posts: 1080 x 1350
- Square carousel interiors: 1080 x 1080
- Slides/video exports: 1920 x 1080
- Stories/Reels covers: 1080 x 1920

**Typography hierarchy:**
- H1: 72–88 pt for 1080 x 1350 hero hook or quote
- H2: 42–54 pt
- Body: 24–28 pt
- CTA: 22–24 pt
- Story H1: 56–68 pt
- Square H1: 60–72 pt

**Rule:** headlines should be two lines max. Body copy should usually stay around 40–60 words max per slide or tile.

### Layout Rules
- Use generous margins and breathing room
- Keep type, icons, and faces out of the outer 6% of the canvas
- Use one focal message per frame
- Avoid crowding
- Preserve safe zones
- Use consistent gutters
- CTA should be clear, singular, and easy to find

### Imagery Rules
**Should feel:** Organic, watercolor, reflective, layered, threshold-oriented, nature-based, symbolic, emotionally meaningful

**Do:**
- Warm and soften photography 15–25% before use
- Place images on Ivory Light or Paper backgrounds, or inside an off-white matte
- Use one focal image and one quiet supporting motif
- Incorporate watercolor textures and gold filigree for mystical elegance
- Use butterfly motifs and sacred geometry as supporting elements

**Avoid:**
- Neon filters
- Pure black backdrops
- Oversaturated gradients
- Generic smiling stock coaching images
- Crowded multi-photo layouts
- Canva glow effects
- WordArt
- Bevels
- Heavy shadows
- Random fonts or stock icons

### Logo Rules
- Use clear space around the LifeCharter wordmark
- Minimum digital width: 160 px
- Minimum print width: 1.1 inches
- Preferred backgrounds: Ivory Light or Paper
- When placing over imagery, use an Ivory Light panel or 80–90% wash
- Never stretch, outline, bevel, glow, rotate, or recolor outside approved palette

### Graphic Motifs & Visual Language (LifeCharter Brand)
**Core Motifs:**
- Butterfly line art and flourishes
- Kaleidoscope / mandala emblems
- Gold filigree flourishes and decorative linework
- Watercolor texture washes
- Sacred geometric linework
- Connector lines with gold nodes

**Icon + Shape Language:**
- Rounded panels with thin gold outlines
- Circular icon badges and medallion styling
- Gold arrows and pathway connectors
- Dotted gold rings
- Pathway tile styling for sequences

### Final Creative Test
Before any asset is approved, ask:
- Does this feel like sacred clarity, not visual noise?
- Is the background Ivory Light, Paper, or another approved color?
- Is body copy Deep Indigo or Ink?
- Are the fonts elegant, readable, and limited to two families?
- Is there one clear message?
- Is the CTA singular?
- Are margins preserved?
- Does the image feel warm, mystical, and symbolic?
- Is the logo given enough breathing room?
- Do gold accents feel elegant, not overwhelming?
