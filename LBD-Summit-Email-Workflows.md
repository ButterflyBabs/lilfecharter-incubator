## WORKFLOW 1: Immediate Registration Confirmation

### Email 1A: Free Registration Confirmation
**Trigger:** Person registers for Free Summit Registration  
**Send:** Immediately  
**Subject Options:**
- You're in! Your Life by Design Summit seat is saved
- Your next chapter has a seat waiting
- Welcome to Life by Design, your free summit access is confirmed

**Purpose:** Confirm registration, build trust, set expectations

**Core Message:**
- Confirm they are registered
- Remind them of summit dates and times
- Introduce the heart of the summit: clarity, purpose, aligned action
- Invite them to mark their calendar
- Softly introduce the Next Chapter Pass and Integration Pass

**Primary CTA:** Add to calendar  
**Secondary CTA:** Explore pass options

## Recommended Automation Structure

### Automation 1: Summit Registration Router
**Trigger:** Registration form submitted  
**Condition:** Pass selected

- If Free → Add LBD-Free-Registration
- If Next Chapter → Add LBD-Next-Chapter-Pass
- If Integration → Add LBD-Integration-Pass

Then add everyone to:
- LBD-Summit-Registered
- Main pre-summit nurture sequence

---

### Automation 2: Integration Pass Bump-Up
**Trigger:** Free or Next Chapter registration  
**Condition:** Does not have LBD-Integration-Pass

Send:
1. Integration intro email
2. Integration education email
3. Replay/resource practical email
4. Fast-action pricing reminder
5. Summit-start price increase reminder

Stop this automation immediately if they purchase Integration Pass.

---

### Automation 3: Integration Pass Buyer Sequence
**Trigger:** Purchase Integration Pass  
**Actions:**
- Remove from bump-up sequence
- Add LBD-Integration-Pass
- Send confirmation
- Send usage guide
- Send resource/workbook access
- Add to Integration daily reminders
- Add to post-summit credit sequence

**Segment:** Dynamic content by pass level

### Automation 5: Post-Summit Conversion Sequence
